My teaching style

I consider teaching as an active exercise. Every student poses a new problem. What do I mean by that? Every one of us have an unique way understanding things and an unique set of learned knowledge. Which means I need to focus on every one of my student to help them understand the concept that I'm trying to teach. So because of this the number of students in my online class will be restricted to 6.

This helps you to ask questions when needed without hesitation and interruption of the entire class because there will be atmost 6 of you! This also gives me a chance to evaluate the progress that you are making during the class itself and adjust the pace accordingly.

Uniqueness about this approach

In a typical classroom online or offline students are overcrowded. Despite the best efforts of the teacher individual attention is not possible. This forces the teacher to go by the script except for one or two students who are exceptional in that particular day!

Why 6 per class?

It is better than 50 per class but more affordable than 1 on 1 coaching. It also provides an environment where students can interact among themselves, help one another with reduced stress in some cases. Take for example, like I said before every one of us has our own perspective/way of looking at this world. Your fellow student might ask a question or provide a solution that you never even thought about. Group classes has it's advantage too!
